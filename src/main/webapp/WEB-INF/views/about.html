About

EscoTech was founded in 2020 during the COVID 19 pandemic to allow people to escape to a time before or after the pandemic - to a time when the economy is open and people could work. This allowed non-essential workers a means to still earn a living, so they could support their households. Mr. Escobar was determined to offer people an affordable way to get back to work and keep the economy going.

The People

Govinda Escobar is the owner and sole employee of EscoTech. He has decades of experience creating and repairing time machines. He holds a in physics from UCLA and a degree in computer science from ASU. His interests include cosmology, astronomy, and software engineering. His hobbies include weight lifting, meditation, yoga, dancing, cooking, and attending cultural events.
