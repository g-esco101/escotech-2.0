Time machine creation and repair

EscoTech

EscoTech is a cutting-edge tech company that specializes in the creation of phone-booth style time machines as well as the repair of any type of time machine. The time machines are created and repaired solely by Govinda Escobar. He has been studying physics and computer science since he was a child. He made his first time machine in high school with friends Bill and Ted for a history project, in which they traveled back in time to interview historical figures such as Abraham Lincoln and Genghis Khan.
